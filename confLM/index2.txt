<br><br>This event is one of the activities taking place at Galileo
Galilei Institute, in Florence, in the framework of the two month
workshop on `Statistical Mechanics, Integrability and
Combinatorics'. It aims at bringing together mathematicians and
physicists interested in problems at the interface between statistical
mechanics, combinatorics and probability theory. 

<br>
<br>

In this Focus Week, emphasis is put on lattice models, and their
behaviour at a macroscopic level (scaling limits at criticality) and
especially at a microscopic level (combinatorial and algebraic
structures).
<br>
<br>
<br>

Main topics:
<br>
<br>- Discrete holomorphicity and integrability 
<br>- Lattice models and combinatorics 
<br>- Quantum integrability and  correlation functions. 
<br>



<br><br> Deadline for application: May 1st, 2015.
