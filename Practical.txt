GGI-agreed accomodations:

<br><br> 

- The complete list of GGI-agreed
accommodations is available <a href="./Accommodations.pdf">here</a>.

<br>

- GGI agreed accommodations may offer you the special GGI
prices shown in the list (and in case of GGI financial support, accept
to be paid directly by GGI), only if the reservation is made through
the GGI accomodation staff.  

<br>

- To receive help from the GGI accomodation staff, you need to fill the
<a
href="https://www.ggi.fi.infn.it/apply/index.php?page=apply.inc&id=139">online
registration form</a>, first.

<br>
- Only after registration, and after receiving GGI official invitation letter,
please contact the GGI accomodation staff,
in particular, Mrs. Mirella Ridi at <a
href="mailto:event@youritalianplanners.com">Studio Guidi</a>.
